Our Love Story

Some glimpses into the important phases in the lives of our beloved couple.

August
2011

The first meet

It was the first day of college. Sathish had studied in an all boys school and Swathiya from an all girls school.
Being new to the college atmosphere, everyone in college was looking out for a cute person from the opposite sex.
That is when a professor called out Sathish to the centre of the class and complimented him on his professional dressing style.
This impressed Swathiya.

April 24
2012

The Proposal

Over time, Swathiya and Sathish became the best of friends and were really possessive about each other. There wasn't a day that went by without them talking to each other.
One day Sathish decided to give it a go and opened up to Swathiya about his feelings for her. Eventhough they were initially reluctant about this important decision in their lives, after a lot of practical discussions they decided to give it a chance.
The rest is history.

August
2012

First date

After being in a relationship for a few months, they went on their first date to a movie at Escape Cinemas (EA Chennai).
Their love affection for each other grew day by day.

January
2018

Our wedding

Over the years their relationship blossomed into a beautiful one and finally they decided to take the plunge.
Swathiya talked to her parents about Sathish and her parents taking into account their daughter's happiness, accepted it.
Then Sathish did the same and later both their parents met with each other and they decided to proceed with the marriage.
The engagement was fixed for 14th November 2018 and the wedding for 10th March 2019.
It was all a dream come true.
